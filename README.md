### 🧠 The Mission
I am an engineering student and solo founder focused on building scalable, high-performance applications with clean, minimal architectures. My primary domains include local AI models, quantitative development, and generative UI.

### 🏗️ What I'm Building
*   **[Zenith AI](#)** - Developing a high-performance, Jarvis-like local AI system.
*   **[StudyGigs](#)** - Mobile-first job marketplace connecting college students with short-term gigs in Bengaluru.
*   **[Quant Trading](#)** - Researching and developing algorithmic trading strategies and quantitative finance roadmaps.
